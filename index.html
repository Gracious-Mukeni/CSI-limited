Our Services

At CSI Security Company, we offer a wide range of security solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of your business and home. Our services are designed to provide comprehensive protection and peace of mind.

Cybersecurity Services

  • Network Security: Protecting your network from unauthorized access and attacks.
  • Endpoint Protection: Safeguarding devices like computers, tablets, and phones.
  • Penetration Testing: Simulating cyberattacks to identify vulnerabilities in your systems.
  • Incident Response: Providing immediate support in case of a security breach.
  • Data Encryption: Ensuring your sensitive data remains secure from unauthorized access.

Physical Security Services

  • Access Control Systems: Installing systems like keycards and biometric scanners for secure access.
  • CCTV Installation and Monitoring: Setting up surveillance cameras for real-time monitoring.
  • On-Site Security Personnel: Providing trained security guards for property protection.
  • Alarm Systems: Installing and maintaining alarms for intrusion detection.

IT Support and Maintenance

  • System Monitoring: Monitoring systems for potential security threats.
  • Software Updates and Patch Management: Keeping your systems up to date with the latest security patches.
  • Backup and Recovery Solutions: Setting up data backup systems and recovery plans.
  • Cloud Security: Protecting your cloud-based data and applications from cyber threats.

Compliance and Risk Management

  • Compliance Audits: Ensuring your business meets industry security standards.
  • Risk Assessments: Identifying potential security risks and offering actionable solutions.
  • Policy Development: Helping you create security policies and protocols to safeguard your operations.

Managed Security Services

  • 24/7 Security Monitoring: Constant monitoring of your network and systems for threats.
  • Threat Intelligence: Providing insights into emerging security threats.
  • Managed Detection and Response (MDR): Detecting and responding to security incidents in real-time.
